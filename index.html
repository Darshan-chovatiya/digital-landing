Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our digital card solutions

How does the NFC Smart Card work?
Simply tap your NFC card on any NFC-enabled smartphone, and your digital business card will instantly appear on their device. The card contains a programmable NFC chip that redirects to your personalized digital profile with all your contact information, social media links, and business details.
Is it compatible with all smartphones?
Yes! Our NFC cards work with 99% of modern smartphones including iPhone (iOS 11+) and Android devices (Android 4.4+). For devices without NFC, we also provide QR code alternatives that work universally across all smartphones and tablets.
Can I customize my digital card design?
Absolutely! Our Digital Card App offers unlimited customization options including custom themes, colors, fonts, logos, background images, and layout styles. You can create multiple card designs for different purposes and switch between them instantly.
Do I get analytics on my card performance?
Yes! Our app provides real-time analytics including total views, click-through rates, most popular links, geographic data of viewers, and engagement trends. You can track which networking events or meetings generate the most interest in your profile.
Can I update my card information after it's created?
Of course! You can update your information anytime, anywhere through our mobile app or web dashboard. All changes are reflected instantly across all your shared cards, and the physical NFC card will always redirect to your latest information.
What's included in the ₹999 package?
Our ₹999 package includes the complete Digital Card App with unlimited card creation, all premium themes, real-time analytics, social media integration, lead management, and a FREE premium NFC card with custom design. It's a one-time purchase with lifetime access.
How long does NFC card delivery take?
We deliver across India within 3-5 business days through reliable courier services. You'll receive a tracking number once your card is dispatched. For major cities, express delivery options are available for faster delivery within 1-2 days.
What kind of customer support do you provide?
We offer 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including live chat, email, and phone support. Our technical team provides setup assistance, troubleshooting, design guidance, and ongoing support to ensure you get the most out of your digital card solution.
